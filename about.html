I'm an industrial designer based in Montreal

My goal is to put the user experience of the product at the forefront of any project on which I work. I believe in simple design and in creating satisfying and meaningful interactions forusers.

My aim for any project is to blend minimalistic design with intuitive interactions. I focus largely on prototyping and user testing, in order to determine what appeals most to potential users on both a tactile and aesthetical level.
